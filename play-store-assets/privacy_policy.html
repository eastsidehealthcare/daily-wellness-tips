Our Privacy Commitment

We take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains how the Daily Wellness Tips app handles your information.

What We Do NOT Do

This app is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • NO data collection - We don't collect any personal information
  • NO permissions required - The app doesn't request any permissions
  • NO internet connection - The app works completely offline
  • NO analytics or tracking - We don't track your usage
  • NO advertisements - The app is completely ad-free
  • NO data sharing - We don't share data with third parties
  • NO accounts or login - No user registration required

How the App Works

The Daily Wellness Tips app is a simple, offline application:

  • All wellness tips are stored locally in a JSON file within the app
  • The app selects tips based on the day of the year
  • No data leaves your device
  • No external servers or APIs are contacted
  • The app functions entirely on your device

Information Storage

All data remains on your device:

  • Tips are stored in the app's assets folder
  • No personal data is stored or processed
  • No usage statistics are recorded
  • No preferences or settings are saved (the app has no settings)

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages:

  • No age restrictions
  • No inappropriate content
  • No social features
  • Safe for children and adults alike
